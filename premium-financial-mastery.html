The three numbers that run your business
From the Financial Clarity guide — if you know nothing else, know these.
Gross Margin %
Revenue minus the direct cost of delivering your service or product, expressed as a percentage. The efficiency of your core business model.
If this is falling, you're working harder for less. Price or cost — one of them needs to move.
Cash Runway
How many weeks the business can operate if revenue stops tomorrow. Cash in bank divided by weekly operating cost.
Profitable businesses die from cash problems. This number tells you how exposed you are.
Owner's Real Return
Net profit after paying yourself a proper market salary. Not what's left after you've underpaid yourself for three years.
This is the number that tells you if you have a business or an expensive job.
