Common questions
What's the difference between the foundation tools and the premium stages? ⌄
The foundation tools are the operational engine — processes, coding assistant, audit tools, and tracking. They're included with every account and cover how your business runs day to day. The premium stages are the strategic programs — each one built around a published book, interactive tools, and video content for a specific phase of your business journey. The foundation keeps the engine running. The stages help you grow, survive, and eventually exit well.
Is the $797 lifetime access genuinely lifetime? ⌄
Yes. One payment, permanent access. Every current stage plus Start and Scale when they launch. The price never changes for you regardless of what we add to the platform. Clients who lock in lifetime access in the early days of the platform benefit most — they get everything that comes later at no additional cost.
Can I be in more than one stage at the same time? ⌄
Absolutely. Business doesn't follow a neat sequence. A business that's growing fast (Build) might also hit a cash crisis (Survive). A business preparing to sell (Exit) might also be scaling (Scale). You unlock what's relevant now — and access everything you've unlocked whenever you need it.
How does earning a stage through milestones work? ⌄
Every client works through a milestone journey — completing their knowledge base sections, using the tools, documenting their processes. As you hit the milestones for a stage, that stage unlocks automatically at no cost. It's designed so engaged clients who do the work get rewarded. If you want to skip ahead, you can unlock any stage immediately for $297.
What's Practice Smarts and how does it relate to Knowledgebase.business? ⌄
Practice Smarts is the done-for-you consulting option for businesses that want Roy in the room — bespoke engagements, fortnightly check-ins, and a fully built knowledge base delivered for you. Knowledgebase.business is the done-with-you platform — the tools, the frameworks, and the support to build it yourself with Roy's guidance. Same methodology, different levels of involvement. Some clients start on the platform and later engage Practice Smarts for a deeper engagement. Others do Practice Smarts first and use the platform for the ongoing rhythm afterwards.
