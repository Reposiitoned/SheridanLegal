Common questions
What's the difference between the foundation tools and the premium stages? ⌄
The foundation tools are the operational engine — processes, coding assistant, audit tools, and tracking. They're included with every account and cover how your business runs day to day. The premium stages are the strategic programs — each one built around a published book, interactive tools, and video content for a specific phase of your business journey. The foundation keeps the engine running. The stages help you grow, break through the ceiling, and eventually exit well.
Is there a lock-in period or minimum commitment? ⌄
No lock-in. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of the billing period. There are no cancellation fees and no minimum term — the program works because it delivers value, not because we make it hard to leave.
Can I enter any program directly, or do I have to start at the beginning? ⌄
Yes. You subscribe at the tier that matches where you are right now — and upgrade when you're ready to move to the next program. Each tier includes everything below it, so upgrading never loses you access to what you already have. Ceiling and Exit are direct-entry programs — you don't need to work through Start or Build to access them.
What happens if I want to upgrade from one program to the next? ⌄
Upgrading is straightforward — your subscription moves to the next tier and you get immediate access to the new program content. You keep everything from your current tier. The price difference is what you pay going forward. There are no lock-in periods — you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.
What's Practice Smarts and how does it relate to Knowledgebase.business? ⌄
Practice Smarts is the done-for-you consulting option for businesses that want Roy in the room — bespoke engagements, fortnightly check-ins, and a fully built knowledge base delivered for you. Knowledgebase.business is the done-with-you platform — the tools, the frameworks, and the support to build it yourself with Roy's guidance. Same methodology, different levels of involvement. Some clients start on the platform and later engage Practice Smarts for a deeper engagement. Others do Practice Smarts first and use the platform for the ongoing rhythm afterwards.
