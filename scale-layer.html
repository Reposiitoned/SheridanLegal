Common questions
Do I have to buy the sections in order? ▾
No. You can unlock any available section at any time. The milestone system rewards clients who work through the program in sequence — but if you need Exit preparation urgently, you can unlock it immediately regardless of where you are in the milestones.
What happens when Start and Scale launch? ▾
If you have lifetime access at $797, they unlock automatically — no extra charge, no action required. If you've purchased individual sections, you can add Start and Scale at $297 each when they launch.
Can I earn sections without paying? ▾
Yes. Build, Survive, and Exit can all be earned by completing milestone tasks — no payment required. The milestone system is the free path. The $297 purchase is for clients who want immediate access without working through the milestone sequence.
Is the $797 really lifetime? ▾
Yes. One payment, permanent access to all current and future Scale Layer sections. As more books are written and more sections are added, the lifetime deal becomes more valuable. The price never increases for lifetime access holders.
What is the difference between the Scale Layer and a Practice Smarts engagement? ▾
The Scale Layer gives you the frameworks, tools, and content to work through the program yourself — with support from the platform. A Practice Smarts engagement puts Roy and the team inside your business — running the discovery, building the portal, and doing the work with you. Scale Layer is done-with-you. Practice Smarts is done-for-you.
🔍
Want someone in the room with you?
The Scale Layer is the self-directed program. Practice Smarts is the bespoke consulting engagement — Roy and the team working inside your business to build the knowledge base, prepare for sale, or structure for scale. One product serves hundreds of clients. The other serves you specifically.
