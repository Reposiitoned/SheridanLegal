About this tool

This tool uses AI to apply your firm's trust accounting rules to incoming payments. It is a decision-support tool only — it does not replace the judgment of a qualified practitioner or the requirements of the Legal Profession Uniform Law (LPUL). When in doubt, always refer to your principal or your state's trust accounting guidelines.

Rules and history are stored locally in this browser. Each device maintains its own copy.
