The Inputs

Five Variables.
Complete Financial Picture.

For each lawyer or partner in your firm, the model takes five numbers and produces a full annual and monthly P&L — instantly, interactively, with no spreadsheet skills required.

Charge-Out Rate

$150 – $1,200/hr

The hourly rate each lawyer charges clients. Model the impact of rate increases before you quote them — see the revenue effect in real time.

e.g. $450/hr × 1,200hrs = $540,000 gross
Billable Hours

0 – 2,200 hrs/yr

Target annual or monthly billable hours. See exactly how utilisation changes the entire P&L — and what the break-even hour count is.

Industry target: 1,800hrs/yr (vs. 220 working days)
Lawyer Salary

$20K – $500K/yr

The base salary cost of the fee-earner including superannuation and oncosts. The model isolates exactly how many hours are needed just to recover this.

e.g. $180,000/yr requires 400hrs at $450/hr to break even
Legal Support

$0 – $100K/yr

Administrative and paralegal help to complete files. Often invisible in standard accounts — this model surfaces its real cost per fee-earner.

Allocated per lawyer based on actual support usage
Annual Overhead

$20K – $400K/yr

Each lawyer's share of rent, software, insurance, and firm running costs. The true cost of keeping a seat warm — visible for the first time.

Model annual or switch to monthly view instantly

The Outputs

What You'll Know
That You Don't Know Now

  • Gross Revenue — Annual & Monthly

    Charge-rate times billable hours, broken into annual and monthly views. Switch modes with one click to match how you mentally model your business.

  • Net Profit & Margin

    Revenue minus all costs — salary, support, and overhead — surfacing the true margin per lawyer. Colour-coded: green means strong, red means act now.

  • Break-Even Hours

    The exact number of billable hours needed to cover every dollar of cost. Know before a matter opens whether a fee-earner can hit the number.

  • Utilisation Rate

    Actual hours vs. the 1,800-hour industry benchmark. Instantly shows whether each lawyer is over-, under- or right-on pace for the year.

  • Effective Daily Rate

    What a lawyer generates per working day at their current rate and hours. A powerful number for communicating value and setting targets.

  • Surplus Profit Hours

    Hours billed beyond break-even — pure profit generation. This is the number that shows who your real profit drivers are.
